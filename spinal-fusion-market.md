<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spinal Fusion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF)</li><li>Anterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (ALIF)</li><li>Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF)</li><li>Posterior Lumbar Fusion (PLF)</li><li>Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal fusion market consists of various surgical techniques aimed at stabilizing the spine. Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF) involves access through the foramen, while Anterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (ALIF) approaches from the front. Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF) accesses the spine from the back, and Posterior Lumbar Fusion (PLF) focuses on fusing the vertebrae without interbody devices. Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF) takes a lateral approach, offering minimal disruption to back muscles, enhancing recovery.</p></p>
