<p><strong>The Radar Level Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact Radar Level Gauge</li><li>Guided Wave Radar Level Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radar level gauge market comprises two primary types: non-contact radar level gauges and guided wave radar level gauges. Non-contact radar level gauges utilize electromagnetic waves to measure liquid or solid levels from a distance, ideal for high-temperature or high-pressure applications. In contrast, guided wave radar level gauges use a probe that transmits and receives radar signals, providing precise measurements even in challenging conditions. Both types offer advantages in various industries, including oil and gas, food and beverage, and water management.</p></p>
