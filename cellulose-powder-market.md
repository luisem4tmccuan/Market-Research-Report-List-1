<p><p>Cellulose powder is a fine, white powder derived from the natural polymer cellulose, found in the cell walls of plants. As a non-toxic and biodegradable material, cellulose powder is gaining popularity as a sustainable alternative to synthetic additives.</p><p>The Cellulose Powder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period.
