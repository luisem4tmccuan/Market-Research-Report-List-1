<p><strong>The Powder Polyester Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Powder Polyester Resins market is categorized primarily into Hydroxyl Terminated and Carboxyl Terminated types. Hydroxyl Terminated resins feature reactive hydroxyl groups, enhancing adhesion and flexibility, making them ideal for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, Carboxyl Terminated resins possess carboxylic acid groups, promoting strong chemical bonding and cross-linking, commonly utilized in thermosetting applications. Both types are crucial for various industrial applications, providing durability, resistance, and versatility in product formulations across sectors like automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
