<p><p>The automotive condition monitoring system market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based systems and local systems. Cloud systems leverage remote servers to collect and analyze vehicle data in real-time, enabling manufacturers and users to access information from anywhere, enhancing predictive maintenance and operational efficiency. Both systems aim to optimize vehicle performance and ensure safety through timely maintenance interventions.</p></p>
