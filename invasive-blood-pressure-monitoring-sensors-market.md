<p><p>The invasive blood pressure monitoring sensors market is categorized into three main types: single channel, dual channel, and triple channel sensors. Single channel sensors measure blood pressure from one site, making them suitable for basic monitoring needs. Dual channel sensors provide simultaneous readings from two sites, enhancing accuracy during critical care. Triple channel sensors allow for three simultaneous measurements, offering comprehensive monitoring for complex cases. Each type caters to varying clinical requirements, ensuring precise and continuous patient monitoring.</p></p>
