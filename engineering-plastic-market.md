<p><p>The engineering plastics market is categorized into two main types: general engineering plastics and special engineering plastics. General engineering plastics, such as nylon and polycarbonate, are widely used for a range of applications due to their durability, light weight, and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, special engineering plastics, like polysulfone and polyetheretherketone (PEEK), are designed for high-performance applications, featuring superior thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties. This differentiation caters to specific industries requiring advanced material solutions.</p></p>
