<p><p>The IPTV set-top box market encompasses various distribution methods. OTT (Over the Top) delivers content via internet streaming, bypassing traditional cable or satellite networks, appealing to users seeking on-demand access. DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission) transmits digital signals through terrestrial broadcast, allowing viewers to receive channels directly without satellite or cable subscriptions. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, offering diverse content access and viewing experiences while reflecting the ongoing evolution in how audiences engage with media.</p></p>
