<p><p>The Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market comprises two primary types: Hybrid Navigation and Electromagnetic systems. Hybrid Navigation combines traditional imaging techniques with electromagnetic tracking, enhancing precision and offering real-time anatomical visualization during surgery. On the other hand, Electromagnetic systems use magnetic fields to track surgical instruments and patient anatomy without requiring line-of-sight, providing greater flexibility in complex procedures. Both types improve surgical accuracy and efficiency, making them valuable tools in modern operating rooms.</p></p>
