<p><strong>The Intravenous (IV) Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Parenteral Solution</li><li>Peripheral Parenteral Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravenous (IV) solution market is primarily categorized into Total Parenteral Nutrition (TPN) and Peripheral Parenteral Nutrition (PPN). TPN delivers all essential nutrients directly into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system, and is suitable for patients with severe gastrointestinal issues or those unable to eat. PPN, on the other hand, provides partial nutrition through a peripheral vein and is typically used for patients who can tolerate some oral intake but require additional nutritional support. Both types are critical for patient care in various medical settings.</p></p>
