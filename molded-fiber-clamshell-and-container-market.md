Transfer molded containers utilize a pressure-molding method, enhancing structural integrity. Thermoformed products are created by heating a fiber sheet, allowing for versatile shapes and designs. Processed pulp refers to using treated pulp to produce lightweight, sustainable packaging. Each type caters to different packaging needs, emphasizing strength, versatility, and eco-friendliness.</p></p>
