<p><p>The oncology molecular diagnostics market encompasses several key testing methods. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies DNA for mutation detection, while INAA (Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Test) provides rapid results for specific target DNA. DNA sequencing and NGS (Next-Generation Sequencing) analyze genetic variations comprehensively. In situ hybridization allows visualization of specific nucleic acids within tissue samples, aiding in cancer localization. DNA microarrays enable simultaneous analysis of thousands of genes. Other methods include chip-based assays and liquid biopsies, enhancing diagnostic precision.</p></p>
