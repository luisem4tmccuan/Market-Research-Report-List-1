<p><p>The stainless steel valve tag market encompasses various types tailored for specific valve applications. Stainless Steel Gate Valve Tags are used for identifying gate valves, which control flow in pipelines. Stainless Steel Ball Valve Tags denote ball valves that manage flow with a spherical disc. Stainless Steel Globe Valve Tags mark globe valves, regulating flow with a movable disk. Stainless Steel Butterfly Valve Tags identify butterfly valves that use a rotating disk to control flow. The "Other" category includes tags for specialized or less common valve types.</p></p>
