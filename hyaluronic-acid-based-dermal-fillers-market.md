<p><p>The hyaluronic acid-based dermal fillers market can be categorized into two main types: single-phase products and duplex products. Single-phase products consist of a uniform gel that provides smooth consistency and easy injection, making them popular for facial volume and wrinkle treatment. Duplex products, however, feature a combination of different gel phases, offering enhanced viscosity and lifting capacity. This dual structure allows for greater customization in treatment, making duplex fillers suitable for more specific applications like defining facial contours or enhancing lip volume.</p></p>
