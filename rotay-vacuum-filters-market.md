<p><p>Rotary vacuum filters are sophisticated filtration systems utilized primarily in industrial applications for solid-liquid separation. They consist of a rotating drum covered with a porous medium, where a vacuum is applied to draw liquid through the filter while retaining solids on the surface. These filters are increasingly employed in industries such as mining, food processing, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment due to their efficiency and ability to handle high volumes of material.</p><p>Market growth analysis reveals a steady rise in demand for rotary vacuum filters, driven by the expanding industrial sector and the need for efficient filtration solutions.
