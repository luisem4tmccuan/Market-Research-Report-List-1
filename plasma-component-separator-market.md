<p><p>The Plasma Component Separator market encompasses several key techniques for isolating plasma components. Membrane plasma separation utilizes selective membranes to separate plasma proteins based on size and charge, offering high purity. Centrifugation involves spinning blood samples at high speeds to separate components based on density, effectively isolating plasma. Filtration employs porous materials to separate plasma from cellular components, facilitating the extraction of specific elements. Each method provides distinct advantages, catering to varying applications in healthcare and research settings.</p></p>
