<p><ul><li>Single-head Contrast Injector</li><li>Dual-head Contrast Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Contrast Injector Market comprises two main types: Single-head and Dual-head Contrast Injectors. Single-head Contrast Injectors are designed for simple applications, utilizing one injector head to administer contrast agents during imaging procedures. In contrast, Dual-head Contrast Injectors offer enhanced efficiency, allowing for simultaneous administration of different contrast agents or doses, thereby improving workflow in imaging centers. Both types cater to varying demands in diagnostic imaging, with dual-head models often preferred for advanced imaging techniques requiring precision and speed.</p></p>
