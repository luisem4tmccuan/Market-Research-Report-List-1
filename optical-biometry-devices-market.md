<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical biometry devices market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact devices. Contact type devices require direct positioning on the eye, allowing precise measurements of ocular parameters, crucial for procedures like cataract surgery. In contrast, non-contact type devices use light to assess eye dimensions without direct contact, providing a more comfortable experience for patients. Both types serve essential roles in ophthalmology, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient care in eye-related treatments and surgeries.</p></p>
